Sumi and the Soulless Spirits

Description: The world of magic is crumbling, which is causing mayhem among the fairies who guard the enchanted forest. You are an apprentice mage who is guided by the different fairy clans to learn and master the wizardry elements. By gathering their elemental orbs, perhaps peace can be restored once again.

Role: Platform & Shader Programmer

  • Used 3D vector math, a line simplification algorithm , and bezier curves to create and render 2D platforms
  • Created shaders for our 3D environmental artist to achieve a visual style that fits well between 3D and 2D art
    • Custom lighting & shadows (for Unity's URP pipeline)
    • Vertex animations for making trees/plants comes to life
    • Flat shading using lighting ramp textures
    • Rim lighting
    • Luminescent flowers

Team size: 5 total, 2 programmers
Engine: Unity C#
Platform: Windows/Mac
